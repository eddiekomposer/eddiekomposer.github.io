UrbanKite

This project aims to envision a valuable new business opportunity that leverages drone and other emerging technologies within the next 10 years. UrbanKite is a drone signage system that provides high quality dynamic display for traffic control and large public event use.

Traffic jams are painful for both drivers
and police officers.


INTRODUCING

Flying Drone Signage System

UrbanKite contains two drones and a fast charging station that can be installed on top of the vehicle. Based on drone technology and rear-projection technology, UrbanKite flexibly delivers diverse customizable messages to any desired position with simple and fast operations.

Compatible with 
complex traffic situations

Broken Traffic Lights

Accident Scene Control

Temporary Traffic Control

Save Human Resource

For a typical traffic at a busy highway intersection caused by a car accident:

Without UrbanKite, it will need:

At least 4 police officers
Countless roadblocks and signs
Expensive mobile message boards and temporary traffic lights

Now with UrbanKite, it will only need:

UrbanKite and 1 officer to set up the system
