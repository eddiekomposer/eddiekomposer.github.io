Ford Infotainment

Background
In collaboration with Ford designers, we envisioned the lifestyle of people in 2035 and created a concept design for the future center console.

This design concept is based on a future where vertical agriculture is highly developed, leading to an increased demand for fresh foods. As a result, there is significant value in trucks and SUVs offering advanced storage solutions, including temperature and humidity control. This is particularly crucial, considering the prevalence of trucks used for delivering food raw materials.

AR Sound Visualization

Background
In contrast to traditional screens, AR incorporates space information, opening up new possibilities for human-computer interactions and transforming user experiences. As a designer, I have delved into UI/UX design in the realm of AR/VR to prepare for the future and embrace the potential of this innovative technology.
Concept
Visualizing sounds has proven to be an effective approach in crafting tangible and responsive experiences. AR amplifies the potential for captivating sound visualization, making it even more intriguing. In this prototype, we utilize loudness, frequency, and phase to govern the animation's strength, color, and position. This innovative approach not only enhances the animation's delightfulness but also adds functional value to the user experience.
