LightBox Live

LightBox Live is a leading real estate information and technology platform. Through operational excellence and a passion for innovation, LightBox facilitates transparency, efficiency, insight, and prediction for real estate investment and location analytics.  Its extensive customer base encompasses commercial and government agencies in need of reliable real estate data and powerful workflow solutions. These clients include brokers, developers, investors, lenders, insurers, technology providers, environmental consultants, and valuation professionals.

High Level Goals

The ultimate goal of LightBox Live is to offer an all-in-one platform that empowers brokers and investors to optimize and automate their decision-making and execution processes. This comprehensive solution aims to help them thrive in the highly challenging commercial real estate market. By providing a suite of powerful tools and features, LightBox Live enables its users to achieve success with greater efficiency and confidence.
