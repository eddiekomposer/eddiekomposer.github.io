LightBox Dev Portal

LightBox Dev Portal is for developers to access our developer tools, API documentation, and learn about various use cases. In the latest update, we completely revamped the visual design to align with our modern and cohesive design language. One of the innovative features we introduced is the console, which provides developers with instant access to the information they need, eliminating the need to search through lengthy documentation page by page.

Background

https://www.lightboxre.com/data/

In addition to our SaaS products, LightBox is also a data company that offers a diverse range of services, including APIs, bulk files, SaaS applications, and third-party data, all presented in a consistent data model.

The LightBox Developer Portal plays a crucial role in facilitating our clients' connection to this extensive universe of data, empowering them to leverage cloud-based APIs for their analysis and workflows. This portal is integral to delivering a superior user experience.

Goal

The current design is mediocre, and our approach goes beyond mere visual updates. Instead, we are starting over and reimagining how developers work. Our emphasis is on aligning our design with users' personas and preferences, ensuring a user-centric experience that caters specifically to their needs and expectations.
