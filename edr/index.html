LightBox EDR

LightBox EDR (Environmental Design & Research) delivers the highest quality data in the industry, in the fastest turnaround, along with innovative workflow tools for quality, collaboration, and efficiency. With over 2,000 databases, and the nation’s largest collection of historical resources, LightBox EDR puts the power of quality data and insight into the hands of the industry’s most successful site assessors.

High Level Goals

Powered by the industry’s leading government records report and a full suite of historical resources, the ultimate goal is to make accessing comprehensive environmental due diligence information fast, easy, & cost effective.

Learn more about what our users do

An environmental site assessment (also known as ESA, environmental due diligence and all appropriate inquiry) is commonly done prior to commercial or industrial property transactions to assess the likelihood of contamination and the liability for cleanup if contamination is found.

Define the target site, area of concern, adjoining area etc

Review all available database records, government permits etc to find potential interesting deatils

Review historical maps, events, and occupants

Generate findings, drawings and reports
